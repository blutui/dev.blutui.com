## Create a new Role 

This is where you create a new Role.

![Role section](https://cdn.blutui.com/uploads/assets/Help/agency/organisation-settings/creating-a-role/roles.png?format=webp&width=800)

Click on the `Create role` button on the top right-hand section of the screen.

![Create new role](https://cdn.blutui.com/uploads/assets/Help/agency/organisation-settings/creating-a-role/create-role.png?format=webp&width=800)

You can now select the appropriate permissions for `Read`, `Write`, `Delete` etc.
