## Team

This is where you invite your team.

![Team section](https://cdn.blutui.com/uploads/assets/Help/agency/organisation-settings/inviting-your-team/team.png?format=webp&width=800)

You send an invite by typing in the `Email address` of the person you are trying to invite into your team. They will recieve an email with a link to join the team.

![Send invitation](https://cdn.blutui.com/uploads/assets/Help/agency/organisation-settings/inviting-your-team/sendinvite.png?format=webp&width=800)

Select the correct `Role` for the invite, these couls be `Owner`, `Admin`, `Developer` etc.
