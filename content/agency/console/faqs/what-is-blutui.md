---
title: 'What is Blutui?'
description: 'A description of Blutui'

---

Blutui is the website creation platform built exclusively for creatives like you. Blutui allows agencies and creatives to build sites efficiently with total project control and complete creative freedom. With a powerful content management system (CMS) at its core, Blutui has been developed within a digital agency environment to suit creative agency workflows and project management requirements.
