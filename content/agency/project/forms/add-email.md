### Adding email to form

To begin you need to have a verifed email to add to do this go to [this page in the settings](/dashboard/site-dashboard/settings/user). After you go throw the steps to adding and verifying and email now you can add it to the form. 
 
You can then go to `Form settings` on the form you want to add the email on. You can now add the email after its been verified but adding it to the `Send submissions to` input field in the top right corner.
